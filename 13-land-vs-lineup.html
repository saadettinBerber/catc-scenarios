Land vs Line-Up

An aircraft is cleared to Land while another aircraft is given Line-Up on the same runway

LAND vs LINE-UP

📊 Data Required

Clearances Assigned Runway Holding Point Surveillance

⚠️ General Rule

Important: In each scenario, it is deemed that the first Clearance in the heading title is the one that has been input by the Controller first and the second Clearance triggers the alert.

🚨 Alert Triggered

1 Landing while Line-Up on Same Runway

If IBE789 is given Cleared to Land and KLM789 is given Line-Up on the same runway.

⚠ CONFLICT

Situation 1: IBE789 cleared to Land while KLM789 is given Line-Up on the same runway

2 Landing while Line-Up in Opposite Direction

If IBE789 is given Cleared to Land and IBE987 is given Line-Up on the same runway in the opposite direction.

⚠ CONFLICT

Situation 2: IBE789 cleared to Land while IBE987 is given Line-Up on the same runway in the opposite direction

✅ Exemptions to the Rule

  • 1
    If the position that KLM789 is lining up from is behind the actual position of IBE789, then no alert is triggered. This allows the Controller to maintain a high runway throughput. ✓ NO ALERT
  • 2
    Additionally the use of Conditional Line-Up Clearances will not trigger an alert. However, once an aircraft has completed its Conditional Line-Up, the system must assign the status Line-Up to that aircraft. As a result the aircraft will then be subject to the CATC rules for Line-Up vs Line-Up/Cross or Enter/Take-Off/Land. ✓ NO ALERT
  • 3
    Local procedures may permit the situation where IBE789 has landed and is still on the runway and is moving below a specified speed and is a certain distance from IBE987 and the Controller is confident that the aircraft will vacate before the Line-Up point of IBE987. In this case surveillance, holding point and route are needed to determine whether to trigger an alert or not. ✓ NO ALERT

📋 Quick Reference

Situation Description Alert Exemption
1 Landing while Line-Up on same runway CONFLICT Line-Up from behind landing position; Conditional Line-Up
2 Landing while Line-Up in opposite direction CONFLICT Local procedures (speed, distance, vacating confidence)
