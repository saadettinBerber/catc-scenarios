Line-Up vs Land

An aircraft is cleared to Line-Up while another is cleared to land on the same runway

LINE-UP vs LAND

📊 Data Required

Clearances Assigned Runway Holding Point Surveillance

⚠️ General Rule

Important: In each scenario, it is deemed that the first Clearance in the heading title is the one that has been input by the Controller first and the second Clearance triggers the alert.

🚨 Alert Triggered

1 Land on Same Runway – Same Direction

If IBE987 is given Line-Up and IBE789 is given cleared to land on the same runway.

⚠ CONFLICT

Situation 1: IBE987 at holding point (Line-Up), IBE789 cleared to land on the same runway

2 Land on Same Runway – Opposite Direction

If KLM789 is given Line-Up and IBE789 is given cleared to land on the same runway in the opposite direction.

⚠ CONFLICT

Situation 2: KLM789 at one end (Line-Up), IBE789 landing from the opposite direction

✅ Exemptions to the Rule

  • 1
    The surveillance function and holding point are used to determine whether IBE789 has passed the assigned holding point of IBE987 and if this is the case then ✓ NO ALERT is triggered. This allows the Controller to maintain a high runway throughput.

📋 Quick Reference

Situation Position Alert Exemption

2 LND opposite direction CONFLICT Landing aircraft passed HP
