Cross or Enter vs Land

An aircraft/vehicle is given Cross or Enter while another aircraft is cleared to Land on the same runway

CROSS / ENTER vs LAND

📊 Data Required

Clearances Assigned Runway Holding Point Surveillance

⚠️ General Rule

Important: In each scenario, it is deemed that the first Clearance in the heading title is the one that has been input by the Controller first and the second Clearance triggers the alert.

🚨 Alert Triggered

1 Cross/Enter while Land on Same Runway

If CHECKER1 is given Cross or Enter and AZA456 is given Cleared to Land on the same runway.

⚠ CONFLICT

Situation 1: CHECKER1 given Cross/Enter while AZA456 is cleared to Land on the same runway

✅ Exemptions to the Rule

  • 1
    Surveillance is used to determine if the CROSS/ENTER mobile has vacated the runway protection area, in which case ✓ NO ALERT is triggered.

📋 Quick Reference

Situation Description Alert Exemption
1 Cross/Enter while Land on same runway CONFLICT Cross/Enter mobile vacated runway protection area
