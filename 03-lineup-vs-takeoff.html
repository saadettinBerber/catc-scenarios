Line-Up vs Take-Off

An aircraft is cleared to Line-Up while another is given Take-Off clearance on the same runway

LINE-UP vs TAKE-OFF

📊 Data Required

Clearances Assigned Runway Holding Point Surveillance

⚠️ General Rule

Important: In each scenario, it is deemed that the first Clearance in the heading title is the one that has been input by the Controller first and the second Clearance triggers the alert.

🚨 Alert Triggered

1 Take-Off From Behind or On Runway

If on the same runway, DLH321 is given Line-Up from a holding point and AZA654 is given Take-Off from a position on the runway or from a holding point behind DLH321.

⚠ CONFLICT

Situation 1: DLH321 at holding point (Line-Up), AZA654 given Take-Off from behind

2 Take-Off From Opposite End

If on the same runway, IBE987 is given Line-Up from a holding point and KLM789 is given Take-Off from a holding point at the opposite end of the runway.

⚠ CONFLICT

Situation 2: IBE987 at one end (Line-Up), KLM789 given Take-Off from the opposite end

✅ Exemptions to the Rule

N/A — No exemptions apply to this scenario.
